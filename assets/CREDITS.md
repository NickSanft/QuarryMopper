# Asset Credits

All gameplay assets in this project are CC0 (public domain) unless otherwise
noted. Although CC0 does not require attribution, we track sources here as
good hygiene and to protect against accidental non-CC0 contamination.

## Textures (placeholder art, hand-authored for this project, CC0)

These hand-drawn SVG placeholders are dedicated to the public domain (CC0).
They are slated for replacement by polished CC0 art from OpenGameArt.org
during the final art pass.

- `assets/textures/parchment_bg.svg` — parchment background
- `assets/textures/cell_stone.svg` — hidden cell tile
- `assets/textures/cell_revealed.svg` — revealed cell tile
- `assets/textures/mine_skull.svg` — mine icon (skull)
- `assets/textures/flag_sword.svg` — flag icon (sword in stone)
- `assets/textures/numbers/rune_1.svg` … `rune_8.svg` — adjacency rune glyphs
- `assets/title/wooden_plank.svg` — title screen plank overlay
